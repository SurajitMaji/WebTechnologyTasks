Tribute to A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
About A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
A P J Abdul Kalam the "Missile Man of India" was born on October 15, 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of India and was reowned scientist, known for his work in India's space missile programs.
Achievments
  • Developed India's first satelite launch vehicle(SLV-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
